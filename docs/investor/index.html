Investor overview

Built for launch, designed to grow.

Loom 1.0 has the core life-management system in place, and early support helps fund the deeper intelligence layer.

A live product with room to expand.

Loom starts with a complete iPhone experience and a focused business model, then grows through deeper intelligence, useful automation, and broader platform reach as adoption supports it.

01

Loom on iPhone

Core Loom functionality is live: purpose, fulfillment areas, goals, Action Plans, Little Wins, reflection, capture, and LoomAI support.

02

Flexible plans

Customers can choose Annual with a 7-day free trial, Monthly for flexible access, or Lifetime for a one-time purchase.

03

Product roadmap

Future R&D targets include deeper intelligence, agentic task completion, and Android or web support when iOS adoption supports expansion.

Growth thesis

One system, multiple paths to durable value.

Loom is designed around a recurring daily-use loop: direction, action, small wins, and reflection. The product can grow in usefulness as the system understands more context and helps users act on it.

Clear customer choiceAnnual, monthly, and lifetime options serve different buying preferences.
Focused acquisitionSearch-led education and the ambassador program create practical, trust-based discovery paths.
Expanding product depthLoomAI and agentic workflows can increase the value of the connected life-management graph over time.
