Payment details and qualifications

Estimated subscription rewards use current U.S. list prices ($15 monthly and $79 annual), the audience's 10% discount, and an assumed 15% Apple commission. Monthly: $15 × 90% × 85% × 30% = approximately $3.44. Annual: $79 × 90% × 85% × 30% = approximately $18.13. Actual rewards may vary by storefront pricing, Apple platform fees, taxes, refunds, and chargebacks.

Net revenue means the amount Loom receives after Apple platform fees, taxes collected on Loom's behalf, refunds, and chargebacks. Subscription commission is earned once on the referred customer's first eligible paid monthly or annual transaction; free trials must convert to a paid transaction, and renewals do not earn additional commission. Lifetime referrals pay a separate fixed $30 reward.

Purchases are reviewed for 30 days. Refunded, reversed, fraudulent, self-referred, untracked, or otherwise ineligible purchases do not earn a reward. Approved balances are distributed on a monthly payout schedule after the ambassador reaches a $50 minimum balance. The operative program terms provided to approved ambassadors control if they differ from these examples.

Ambassador program FAQ.

Is the Loom Ambassador Program open?
Yes. Applications are open for creators, coaches, educators, community leaders, and other people with a relevant, engaged audience. Use either Apply now button on this page to submit your application.
How does the 10% audience discount work?
Each ambassador receives plan-specific codes and tracked links. The offer gives eligible customers 10% off annual or monthly payments during their first 12 months, plus 10% off a qualifying one-time lifetime purchase through a unique lifetime code.
How is the 30% commission calculated?
Subscription commission is a one-time payment equal to 30% of the referred customer's first eligible net subscription payment. At current U.S. prices, after the audience's 10% discount and assuming a 15% Apple commission, the estimated one-time payout is about $3.44 for a qualifying monthly referral or about $18.13 for a qualifying annual referral. Monthly renewals do not earn additional commission. Actual payouts may vary with storefront pricing, Apple commission, taxes, refunds, and chargebacks. Lifetime referrals are separate and pay a flat $30.
How is lifetime access handled?
The ambassador's unique lifetime code gives an eligible customer 10% off the one-time purchase. The ambassador reward is a one-time $30 payment, separate from the 30% subscription reward. A purchase qualifies after the 30-day refund and chargeback review period.
Do I need a large social following?
No single channel is required. Loom considers engaged social audiences, email lists, podcast or video reach, and high-trust coaching or membership communities. Audience relevance and trust can outweigh raw size.
Can coaches, educators, and newsletter writers apply?
Yes. The program is designed for creators, coaches, educators, community leaders, podcasters, writers, reviewers, and other people who teach practical ways to live and work with more intention.
Are self-referrals allowed?
No. The program rewards genuine referrals to new paying customers. Self-referrals, code sites, paid-search bidding on Loom brand terms, misleading claims, and spam are not eligible.
