Ambassador program FAQ.

Is the Loom Ambassador Program open?
Not yet. The application CTA will become active when applications open. The page is live now so prospective partners can understand the planned offer and qualification standards.
How does the 10% audience discount work?
Each ambassador receives a unique tracking code and link. The planned offer gives eligible customers 10% off annual or monthly subscription payments during their first 12 months.
How is the 30% commission calculated?
The commission is 30% of eligible net subscription revenue during the referred customer's first 12 months. Net revenue excludes Apple platform fees, taxes collected on Loom's behalf, refunds, and chargebacks.
Why is lifetime access handled separately?
Apple's standard offer codes are designed for auto-renewable subscriptions, while lifetime access is a one-time purchase. When a limited lifetime offer is active, ambassadors may earn a one-time $30 payment for each qualifying purchase made through their link or code. A purchase qualifies after the 30-day refund and chargeback review period.
Do I need a large social following?
No single channel is required. Loom considers engaged social audiences, email lists, podcast or video reach, and high-trust coaching or membership communities. Audience relevance and trust can outweigh raw size.
Can coaches, educators, and newsletter writers apply?
Yes. The program is designed for creators, coaches, educators, community leaders, podcasters, writers, reviewers, and other people who teach practical ways to live and work with more intention.
Are self-referrals allowed?
No. The program rewards genuine referrals to new paying customers. Self-referrals, code sites, paid-search bidding on Loom brand terms, misleading claims, and spam are not eligible.
