FAQ

Is Loom a goal tracker?
Loom tracks goals, but also connects them to Action Plans, Little Wins, life areas, reflection, and LoomAI guidance.
How does Loom help with habits?
Little Wins keep repeat actions connected to the goals and life areas they support.
Is Loom only for big goals?
No. Loom can support major goals, small repeat actions, loose tasks, reflection, and capture.
