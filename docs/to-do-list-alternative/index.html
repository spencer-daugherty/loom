To-do list alternative

Your life is not a to-do list.

A task list can capture what is due. Loom helps connect what is due to why it matters, what goal it supports, and what repeat actions keep progress alive.

Tasks need context

Action Plans can connect to goals, people, places, due dates, attachments, duration, and status.

Habits need meaning

Little Wins turn repeat actions into progress tied to life areas and goals.

Plans need review

Reflection helps you adjust when a list stops matching your real priorities.

Choose Loom if...

  • Your tasks are organized but still feel disconnected.
  • Your goals, habits, notes, and plans live in different places.
  • You want an iPhone-first system instead of another workspace to build.

A to-do list may be enough if...

  • You only need quick capture and due dates.
  • Your goals and habits already work well elsewhere.
  • You do not want broader life planning layers.

FAQ

Is Loom a task manager?
Loom includes task planning through Action Plans, but it is broader than a task manager.
Can Loom replace a to-do list app?
Yes, if you want tasks connected to goals, habits, life areas, and reflection.
Is Loom affiliated with Todoist, Things, TickTick, or other apps?
No. Loom is independent and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by any competitor mentioned.
